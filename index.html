Connecting UWI Commuters for Affordable, Reliable Journeys

Offers a streamlined carpooling platform that connects students traveling similar routes, providing a more reliable and affordable alternative to public transportation. By leveraging the UWI community, the app promotes ride-sharing to reduce commuting costs, decrease travel time, and foster a more sustainable campus environment.

Why RideShare?

Affordable & Reliable

Save time and reduce costs by connecting with students traveling similar routes.

Community-Focused

Built for the UWI community to promote collaboration and support.

Sustainability

Reduce environmental impact through shared transportation.

Our Market

With an estimated 385 to 770 student users at UWI, RideShare offers an affordable, convenient alternative to public transportation.
